Answer Input Cheat Sheet

  • Fractions: use numerator/denominator, e.g.: 22/25
  • Complex: a+bi format, no spaces, e.g.: 22/25-29/25i
  • Lists: separate answers with commas, e.g.: 3,-3,2
  • Intervals: parentheses/unions, e.g.: (-∞,-7)∪(2,∞)
  • Equations: use ^, parentheses for shifts: (x-4)^2-2
  • Square roots: √ or sqrt(), e.g.: √61 or sqrt(61)
